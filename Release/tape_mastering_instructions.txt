DIY Tape Mastering Instructions for Gyro Run

Insert a blank tape into your C2N on your C64. Zero counter then Press RECORD and PLAY on tape.
As soon as the counter has reached 005. Press STOP. Load in GYRO RUN    /TAPE from your master
disk. Then type in RUN. Then press RECORD and PLAY on tape and wait for the tape mastering
to finishe. The C64 will reset once finished with mastering.

Have fun.
